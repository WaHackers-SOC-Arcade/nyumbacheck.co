Tenant Guide

How to Rent a Safe Apartment in Dar es Salaam in 2025

Rental scams in Dar es Salaam cost Tanzanians billions of shillings every year. Fake "dalali" agents, duplicate listings, and phantom landlords are rampant — especially in Masaki, Oyster Bay, and Mbezi Beach. This guide shows you exactly how to protect yourself: what documents to demand, how to cross-check a title deed at the Land Registry, and why you should never pay a deposit before a physical viewing.

Zanzibar Guide

Best Neighborhoods in Zanzibar for Long-Term Rentals

Zanzibar is no longer just a holiday destination — it is becoming one of East Africa's most sought-after residential and investment markets. From the UNESCO heritage streets of Stone Town to the pristine beaches of Nungwi and the kite-surfing haven of Paje, each neighborhood has a distinct character, price point, and rental demand. This guide breaks down what to expect from each area and who each one is best suited for.

Diaspora Investor Guide

How Tanzanians Abroad Can Safely Buy Property Back Home

Investing in Tanzanian property from the UK, USA, or UAE is possible — but the risks are real. Without someone trustworthy on the ground, you can wire money to a phantom seller and have no legal recourse. This guide explains the exact process: how title deed verification works, what rights foreign-based Tanzanians have under land law, and how NyumbaCheck's on-ground verification service protects your capital remotely.
